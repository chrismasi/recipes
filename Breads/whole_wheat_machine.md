# Whole Wheat Bread for Bread Machine
## Ingredients
- 1.25c Warm Water
- 2T Olive Oil
- 0.25c Honey or Sugar
- 3.5c White Whole Wheat Flour
- 0.25c Sunflower, Sesame or Flax Seeds (optional)
- 1.5t Salt
- 1.5t Yeast
## Directions
- Add ingredients to bread machine in order listed.
- Set to 2lb loaf.
