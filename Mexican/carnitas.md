# Pork Carnitas
## Ingredients
- 3T Salt
- 1T Black Pepper
- 4lbs Pork Shoulder (Boneless) cut into 4 inch chunks
- 4T Cooking Fat
- 1 Onion chopped
- 6 Cloves Garlic, minced
- 1t Chili Powder
- .5t Ground Cinnamon
- .5c Green Onion, sliced
- 1 Lime, juiced
## Directions
- Preheat oven to 350F
- Mix 2T salt and black pepper and season pork.
- Heat cooking oil in heavy dutch oven. Brown meat, 3-4min per side, don't crowd. Set aside meat.
- Add onions to same pot and cook until translucent. 4-5min.
- Add Garlic and cook until aromatic, stir consistently. 1min.
- Add 1c water, chili powder, cinnamon. Mix. Add pork back in.
- Turn up to medium-high and bring to boil.
- Add lid and put in oven. Cook for 2.5ish hours (until it easily shreds with a fork).
