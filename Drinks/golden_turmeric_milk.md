# Golden Turmeric Milk
## Ingredients
- 2c Almond or Coconut or Cashew Milk
- 2T Honey or Agave or Maple Syrup
- 1t Turmeric
- 1t Cinnamon
- 1/4t Ginger
- 1/8t Black Pepper
- 1t Vanilla Extract
Optional:
- 1T Almond Butter
- 1/2T Coconut Oil
- Cardamom
- Cloves
## Directions
- Add all ingredients to a pot over medium heat and bring to a simmer.
- Simmer for 10 minutes to let the flavors meld.
- Pour into a mug.
