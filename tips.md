# DIY Flours
## Self Rising Flour
- 1c (120g) All Purpose Flour
- 1.5t Baking Powder
- 0.25t Salt
## Cake Flour
- 1c Flour
- 2T Corn Starch
# Dry Rubs
## Basic
- 3 Tbsp Kosher Salt
- 3 Tbsp Smoked Paprika (or chili powder)
- 2 Tbsp Onion Powder
- 2 Tbsp Garlic Powder (or 1?)
- 2 Tbsp Oregano
- 2 Tbsp Ground Cumin
- 2 Tbsp Brown Sugar
